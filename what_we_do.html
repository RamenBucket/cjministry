What We Do

We bridge the gap for people experiencing homelessness, give weekly check-ins for the elderly, and work through barriers for those who have a mental illness or substance abuse to help them work toward rebuilding themselves and their family's. We travel and want to meet you where you are, allowing us to lead you toward a relationship with Christ while providing these resources within our communities where people are most vulnerable due to lifestyle or situations.
